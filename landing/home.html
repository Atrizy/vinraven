Frequently Asked Questions

How long does setup take? +
Most businesses are live in under 5 minutes. Upload your knowledge base (FAQs, docs, menu, etc.), we train the AI instantly, and you embed one line of code on your site. That's it.
What happens if it can't answer a question? +
VinRaven automatically creates a support ticket and captures the customer's email. You'll get notified instantly so you can follow up personally. No lead gets lost.
Do I need technical skills? +
Not at all. If you can copy and paste, you can install VinRaven. We provide step-by-step setup videos, and our team handles the initial training for you during onboarding.
Can I customize how it looks? +
Yes! Match your brand colors, upload your logo, customize the greeting message, and position the widget anywhere on your site. Professional plan includes full white-label options.
What kind of businesses use VinRaven? +
Restaurants (hours, menu, reservations), medical clinics (appointments, insurance), retail stores (inventory, shipping), salons (booking, services), and any local business that gets repetitive customer questions.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel anytime from your dashboard with one click. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
